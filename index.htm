Employee Spotlight

Chris

President + Founder

Chris brings a deep and eclectic background to Purple Orange. Founding the agency in 2009, Chris previously worked with agencies large and small, and as Marketing Director for both Alpinist Magazine and NEMO Equipment.

Chris has managed North America PR and communication on behalf of dozens of brands ranging from Arc'teryx Equipment to Peak Design and PROBAR. Chris is also the Founder of Visably, a new MarTech software that focuses on integrated SEO strategy and brand measurement.

Chris currently lives in Jackson Hole, WY where he can trail run at lunch and climb and ski in the Tetons year round. He is a published writer, award-winning filmmaker, father, and husband.
